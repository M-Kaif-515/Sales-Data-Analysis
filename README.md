# Sales-Data-Analysis

**This is my first end-to-end Data Analytics project using Excel, SQL, and Power BI.**

🚀**Project Overview:**
      This project focuses on analyzing a large sales dataset using Microsoft Excel, MySQL, and Power BI to uncover valuable business insights.
      The objective was to transform raw sales data into meaningful information that can support data-driven business decisions.

🛠️**Tools & Technologies Used:**
      📑 Microsoft Excel.
      🗄️ MySQL.
      📊 Power BI.
      📈 Data Visualization.
      📉 Business Intelligence.

📂**Project Workflow:**

📑**Excel**
      Imported and reviewed raw sales data.
      Performed data validation and preprocessing.
      Prepared dataset for SQL and Power BI analysis.

🗄️**MySQL**
      Imported 50K+ sales records into MySQL.
      Wrote SQL queries to analyze business performance.
      Performed data aggregation, filtering, sorting, and grouping.
      Generated insights related to sales, profit, customers, products, markets, and shipping modes.

📊**Power BI**
      Created a 3-page interactive dashboard.
   **Designed KPI Cards for:**
       💰 Total Sales.
       💵 Total Profit.
       📦 Total Orders.
       📈 Profit Margin.
       👥 Total Profit.
       📊 Average Sales.
      Added slicers for dynamic filtering.
      Developed charts and visualizations for business analysis.
      Implemented interactive dashboard navigation.

📈**Key Business Insights:**
       🌍**Market Performance**
             APAC emerged as the highest revenue-generating market.
             EU and US followed as strong-performing markets.
       💰**Business Performance**
             Total Sales reached $12.64M.
             Total Profit reached $1.47M.
             Total Orders exceeded 51K+.
       📊**Profitability**
             Overall Profit Margin: 11.61%.
             The business earns approximately $11.61 profit for every $100 in sales.
       🏆**Category Analysis**
             Technology generated the highest profit ($0.66M).
             Office Supplies generated $0.52M.
             Furniture generated the lowest profit ($0.29M).
       👥**Customer Insights**
             Tom Ashbrook was the highest-value customer ($40K Sales).
             Tamara Chand and Greg Tran were among the top-performing customers.
       📦**Product Insights**
             Canon Imageclass 2200 Advanced Copier generated the highest profit ($25K).
             Cisco Smart Phone (Full Size) and Motorola Smart Phone (Full Size) were also top-performing products.
       🚚**Shipping Insights**
             Standard Class was the most preferred shipping mode among customers.

🎯**Business Recommendations:**
      Focus on expanding high-performing markets such as APAC.
      Increase investment in Technology products due to higher profitability.
      Improve profitability of Furniture category through pricing and cost optimization.
      Retain high-value customers through loyalty and engagement strategies.
      Promote top-performing products to maximize revenue and profit growth.
